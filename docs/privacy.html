FairSpin Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

FairSpin is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Household Data: Chore assignments, member names, and completion records are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics services.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your synced data.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Your data remains on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

4. Notifications

FairSpin uses local notifications only to remind you of upcoming chores. We do not send push notifications from any server.

5. Children's Privacy

FairSpin is suitable for family use including children. We do not collect any data from children or adults.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app from your device. This will permanently remove all stored information.
