Frequently Asked Questions

How does FairSpin assign chores fairly?

FairSpin uses a weighted rotation algorithm that considers each chore's effort level and each member's recent workload. Members who have done less recently are prioritized for new assignments.

Can I customize chore effort levels?

Yes! Each chore can be set to Easy (1), Medium (2), or Hard (3) effort level. This ensures that members doing harder chores get appropriate credit.

How do I add family members?

During onboarding, you'll be prompted to add members. You can also add members anytime from the Members tab in the app.

Does FairSpin require a subscription?

No! FairSpin is a one-time purchase of $1.99. No subscriptions, no hidden fees.

Is my data shared with third parties?

Absolutely not. All data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
